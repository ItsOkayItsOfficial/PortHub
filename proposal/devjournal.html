CREATION

Creaing Porthub was difficult. However we had a highly collabrative creative process, which led to many noteworthy ideas. After working out the kinks we felt that we wanted to serve an underserved market. Those learning to code and those needing to get their information online, quickly and easy. The creative process was where we knew we could really shine.

Utilizing a quick wireframe in Draw.io we put our ideas down on paper. Psuedocoding and whiteboading was an invaluable tool. It gave us organization and clarity and focus. It also afforded us the ablitity to define the crucial points in our project.
