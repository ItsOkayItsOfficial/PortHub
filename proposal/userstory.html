PROBLEM?



    THE PROBLEM. New developers face a hurdle of learning the "Why?", before the "How?"
    -How do you create a website showing all the skills you have learned?
    -How do you deploy it?
    -Where do you deploy it?
    -Hosting?
    It is so very common for budding developers to create content to show off their skills. But, it's just as common for them not to know how to get it seen! How can you get your content online? Will it take long? Are there any guides or resources I can use? As developers, the Porthub team slammed into this problem and noticed that others were struggling with it too. Porthub saw this problem and wanted to attack it as developers. We wanted to create a simplistic service, that empowered our user to not only FILL out a completely CUSTOMIZABLE template...BUT ALSO PUBLISH their content on a completely hosted domain! All done in the blink of an eye! A resource for developers, made by developers, to help further development careers.

USE

Learning to code is hard. Finding the perfect resume' tempate is also hard. Putting both skills together to come up with a porfolio that can catch an employers eye? The hardest. We set out to solve that problem. Our development team wanted to create a flawless UI, that not only empowered users but gave them easy-to-follow instructions. Simple steps. Go to our site. Log in, browse resume' templates and select your favorite. Browse portfolio templates and select your favorite. Once completed download a pdf or have a completely customizable portfolio!


SOLUTION


As developers we understood the struggle of creating a portfolio. We understood how much time it took. We understood the pain of knowing that WE ARE NOT DESIGNERS. We created a full scale React Application that solved those needs. A full service that takes content from the user and then PUBLISHES it to a fully hosted domain on Github. All in minutes!!!

While infinitely scalable, we wanted to cater our FOCUS on DEVELOPERS. In specifics, developers in the job market. USE Porthub to show off your skills! Save precious TIME by having all the work done FOR YOU!

Download, Publish all for free! Our development team spent countless hours creating a solution to a major problem. Because we ALWAYS want this tool to be used by anyone who needs it, we have committed to being completely open sourced! Have a good idea for Pothub? Good! Send us a pull request and shoot us some ideas!

We are also MOBILE! Log in to Porthub mobile and see all of your published and saved content LIVE! Quickly update a resume, or tweak your portfolio content on the fly! Impress recruiters and hiring managers with how quick you can adjust your skills!
